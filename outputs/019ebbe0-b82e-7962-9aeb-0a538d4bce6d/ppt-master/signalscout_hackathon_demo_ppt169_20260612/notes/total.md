# 01_cover

Timing: 25 to 30 seconds.

Open with the premise: companies quietly publish buying signals every day through the roles they are hiring for. SignalScout AI turns those public signals into evidence-backed sales priorities for service providers.

Set the scope quickly: this is not a CRM replacement or a lead list marketplace. It is a workflow from job postings to pain, priority, outreach, and team handoff.

# 02_problem

Timing: 30 to 35 seconds.

Frame the common sales problem. Agencies and consultancies already know who they could sell to, but they often do not know who is changing right now or why this week is a good time to reach out.

Static lists age quickly, generic intent data can be broad, and manual research is slow. The opportunity is to turn current public behavior into practical sales action.

# 03_insight

Timing: 35 to 40 seconds.

Make the insight concrete. A job post is more than a recruiting artifact. The role, seniority, department, location, and repeated pattern often point to an operating gap.

Use one or two rows only. RevOps and BI roles suggest reporting pressure; security roles can suggest compliance and access work. SignalScout does not claim guaranteed purchase intent. It gives sellers a better reason to start a timely, specific conversation.

# 04_product_workflow

Timing: 35 to 40 seconds.

Walk through the product system at a high level. SignalScout ingests job data, extracts the hiring signal, scores the lead, drafts the outreach, and sends a Slack digest.

Emphasize artifacts, not architecture: evidence, inferred pain, ranked account, opening line, and team handoff.

# 05_first_user

Timing: 25 to 30 seconds.

Explain the first wedge. The product is built first for agencies, consultancies, and B2B service providers because they sell expertise into companies that are already changing.

Name two examples, then move on. Data-dashboard agencies and AI automation providers both need timely, specific reasons to start conversations. SignalScout anchors that reason in observable hiring behavior.

# 06_product_proof

Timing: 30 to 35 seconds.

Move from concept to product proof. Keep it concrete: 260 postings become a signal feed, Atlas Logistics becomes the top lead, and outreach plus Slack handoff are ready.

Point to the screenshot as product evidence, then immediately tee up the live walkthrough.

# 07_commercial_readiness

Timing: 30 to 35 seconds.

Make the commercial case before switching to the live product. SignalScout is built for B2B agencies and consultancies that need a timely reason to reach accounts already showing operational change.

The marketable wedge is simple: public data, daily priorities, usable sales artifacts, and workflow fit through Slack and exports.

# 08_live_demo

Timing: 15 to 20 seconds.

Transition cleanly. Now switch to the running website and prove the loop: job data to hiring signal to lead score to outreach draft to Slack digest.

Do not add another explanation here. Start the product walkthrough.
